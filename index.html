Reasons to hack on our products

Why should you join us and be a part of PAYMILL open-source community?

Be social and code what could other people help out. Of course we're gonna mention you on our site and make a blog post about that too. We're always looking for talented developers who want to join PAYMILL. Maybe you're the next one?

Beyond those reasons we heart the dev scene out there and want to get all of you working with our code whenever it is possible and on a stable version so you can work without problems on it. And yes, we ♥ animated GIF's as you do, come on guys. Let's talk and have a good time. We are looking forward to hear from you soon!

TL;DR: Just get in touch with us and write a message.
